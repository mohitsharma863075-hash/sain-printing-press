Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our printing services

What is the typical turnaround time for printing orders?

Turnaround time varies based on the project complexity and quantity. Standard business cards typically take 2-3 business days, while large format printing may take 3-5 business days. Rush services are available for urgent projects.

Do you provide design services or only printing?

We offer both design and printing services. Our design team can create custom designs from scratch, or you can provide your own ready-to-print files. We also provide design consultations to ensure optimal print results.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept all standard print file formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. For best results, we recommend PDF files with CMYK color mode and 300 DPI resolution.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services throughout Dadri and surrounding areas. For larger orders, we provide free local delivery. We can also arrange shipping to other locations at additional cost.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, UPI payments, and credit/debit cards. For large orders, we require a 50% deposit upfront with the balance due upon completion.
